Santa's Sweatshop is a Fruit Ninja inspired game encompassing 5 unique gamemodes & silly storyline!

Introduction

Santa's Sweatshop was a 72-hour programming project I undertook for the Winter Melon Jam 2023 with a theme of 'charm'. This involved planning the concept for the game, programming the logic and drawing the pixel art assets.

While it was possible to participate as a team, I chose to go solo in order to build my experience in my first ever game jam! It taught me the importance of time management and the ability to write quality code quickly under pressure.


Guideline

Use your mouse or finger to slash through presents and save Christmas!

The rules for each gamemode are typically self-explanatory, or explained in the Campaign Gamemode. What are you waiting for? Play now!

Notice: It is not recommended to play on mobile and some elements may not display correctly if the screen is too small.

On keyboard only, the button 'L' activates a low detail mode that can improve performance on lower end devices.


Results

This game won Judges' Choice in the Novice section of the competition.
